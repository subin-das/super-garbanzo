welcome to paathalam music band

PAATHALAM isn’t just a music band — it’s a movement of melody, passion, and soul.
Born in the heart of our college, PAATHALAM is a diverse group of student musicians,
who believe in the power of sound to connect, inspire, and transform.

From classical ragas to rock anthems, from soulful ballads to electrifying fusion –
our band breaks boundaries and celebrates every genre. What brings us together is not just talent,
but a shared dream: to create music that speaks, heals, and lives forever.

Whether it's a college fest, a late-night jam session, or a quiet acoustic gig —
PAATHALAM brings energy, emotion, and originality to every stage we step on.

Come, be part of our rhythm. Welcome to the sound of PAATHALAM.
